✨The Inspiration!✨

There's something magical about old photographs – the way they capture a moment, an emotion, a story, all in a single frame. My earliest memory of photography is when I was about 5 or 6 years old. My mother's camera fascinated me, but being a child I was not allowed to use it. I would dream of the day I'd finally get to hold it, but by the time I was old enough, the camera was already damaged

Despite that, my love for photography only deepened. As a child, I was always curious with the photo albums my mom carefully kept in a stack in the shelf. It wasn’t just a collection of pictures; it was a doorway to the past, a silent storyteller that spoke to me in ways words never could.

Now, I realize that my journey with photography began with this childhood curiosity. It wasn’t just about using a camera; it was about preserving memories, connecting with moments, and telling stories that words couldn’t. Photography became my way of expressing emotions, and that's how my passion was born : )
